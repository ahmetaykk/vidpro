Frequently Asked Questions

Which platforms are supported?
VidPro is powered by yt-dlp, which supports 1000+ sites. Preview is optimized for YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok.
How are downloaded files organized?
Files are automatically organized into platform folders (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok). YouTube playlists are also saved into a subfolder named after the playlist.
How do I download audio only (MP3)?
Enable "Audio Only (MP3)" under Download Options to download as MP3.
How do I clip only part of a video?
Open "Advanced Settings" and enter the start and end timestamps (e.g., 00:01:00). This feature is currently supported for YouTube videos only.
How do I avoid downloading the same file again?
When "Skip Existing File" is enabled, already-downloaded files are detected and skipped. The UI will show a warning.
How do I clear job history?
On the Jobs page, use the Clear menu to remove completed, failed, or skipped items individually—or clear all history.
How do I track playlist downloads?
On the Jobs tab, click "Show videos" on a playlist card to see every item, its order, and its current status.
Where are downloaded files saved?
By default, files are saved under /downloads in the project folder, organized into platform subfolders (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok).
How does age-restricted content work?
VidPro automatically extracts and encrypts browser cookies to handle age-restricted content. Simply log in to YouTube in your browser and VidPro will use your authenticated session.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All browser cookies are encrypted with AES-128 and stored locally. No data is transmitted to external servers. Temporary files are securely deleted after use.
How do browser cookies work?
Select your browser in "Cookies from Browser" dropdown. VidPro will extract cookies automatically, encrypt them and cache them for 7 days to minimize security prompts.
What security features are included?
VidPro includes comprehensive security: encrypted cookie storage, input validation, path traversal protection, CORS restrictions, and secure error handling.
How do I use Chrome extension?
Install VidPro extension from chrome-extension/ folder. It detects videos on current pages and sends them to VidPro with one click.
Can I use manual cookies files?
Yes. You can upload a cookies.txt file in Advanced Settings section. The file will be validated and used for authentication.
